## Early-Stage Researchers on knowledge graphs
The <ExternalLink to="https://kg.codezen.net"> KnowGraphs ITN </ExternalLink> offers 15 PhD positions on fundamental and applied research on knowledge graphs. The DICE group will mentor 3 of these ESRs on topics pertaining to <ExternalLink kind="pdf" to="https://www.uni-paderborn.de/fileadmin/zv/4-4/stellenangebote/Kennziffer4024.pdf"> knowledge representation </ExternalLink>, <ExternalLink kind="pdf" to="https://www.uni-paderborn.de/fileadmin/zv/4-4/stellenangebote/Kennziffer4023.pdf"> veracity (also called fact checking) </ExternalLink> and <ExternalLink kind="pdf" to="https://www.uni-paderborn.de/fileadmin/zv/4-4/stellenangebote/Kennziffer4025.pdf"> machine learning </ExternalLink>. Have you received a MSc in Computer Science or in a related area in the last 4 years? Have you been a researcher in Germany for at most one year over the last three years? Does the <ExternalLink to="http://bigchem.eu/node/14"> mobility rule of Marie Curie ITNs </ExternalLink> hold for you? Then apply to the best fitting position below:

     Early Stage Researcher on the veracity of knowledge graphs

## Requirements

Excellent candidates are invited to apply with:

1. Curriculum vitae and copies of degree certificates/transcripts,
2. Writing samples/copies of relevant scientific papers (e.g. thesis),
3. A short statement of research and/or technology interest,
4. Letters of recommendation.

On 19th June 2018, Erwin Hermann presented the research project SOLIDE at the BMBF Innovation Forum "Civil Security" 2018 as part of the session "Operational support - Better management of crisis through intelligent information retrieval." His address included:

- A general overview,
- The consortium partners,
- Main areas of investigation such as (speech recognition, text to speech, ontologies, coreference resolution, disambiguation).

SOLIDE is a joint project of PRO DV (Dortmund), Aristech GmbH (Heidelberg) together with the research group Smart Data Analytics (SDA) of the University of Bonn and the Data Science Chair (DICE) of the University of Paderborn.

The fourth [LIMBO](https://www.limbo-project.org/) face to face meeting happened on October 23rd at the University of Paderborn. The aim of the meeting was to discuss the current state of all work packages and to coordinate the next steps of the Project.

Among others following topics were discussed:

- Integration of quality assurance in the lifting process.
- Integration of ALKIS data
- Mapping of APIs
- Current state of the implementation of all Use Cases
- Exploration and export of data
- Talk with us about possibilities to make use of open data for your mobility or transport problems!!!
